About This Demo

Our demonstration shows how our deep learning techniques compare against humans
for the particular social problem of lost cats,
a problem perceived as a serious problem in Japanese society.

Human

Experience the challenge of discerning subtle differences between stray and pet cats, and see how accurately you can make this distinction.

Deep Learning Model

This model, a fine-tuned ResNet-50, has been specifically adapted for the task of distinguishing between stray and pet cats.

What We Want You To Do

01

Select cat images.

You will choose 10 cat images from a provided set.

02

Classify each image.

You will classify each selected image as either a "Stray Cat" or "Pet Cat" by pressing the corresponding button.

03

Explain the cat facial features focused on during classification.

You will complete a questionnaire explaining the important features of the cat's face you used for classification.

In The Background

While you classify cat images,
our system's deep-learning model will concurrently classify the same images in the background.

Once the survey is submitted, the AI classification results, along with their respective accuracy rates, will be displayed.

Let's get started with classifying stray and pet cats!
